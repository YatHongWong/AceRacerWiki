About Ace Racer

Summary

Ace Racer is an online free-to-play racing game developed and published by NetEase Games and is available for IOS and Android. It was released globally on the 16th of March 2023.

Gameplay

Ace Racer features an arcade racing gameplay. Its most unique features are the inclusion of car abilities, and class-based team gameplay. In this game players must work together in teams of 3, there are 3 classes of cars: speedster, interceptor, supporter. Each class has different abilities (ultimates). The speedster class ultimates are focused on increasing its own speed, trying to get first place. The interceptor class ultimates are focused on annoying the enemy cars, slowing them down. The supporter class ultimates are focused on providing ultimate charge to teammates and increasing their speed.

There are a huge variety of cars in Ace Racer, some are fictional - designed by the Ace Racer team, and some are real and licensed brands.
